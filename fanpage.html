Bob Marley Fan Page

Bio

Robert Nesta Marley OM was a Jamaican singer, songwriter, and musician. Born on February 6, 1945, in St. Ann Parish, Jamaica. Considered one of the pioneers of reggae, his musical career was marked by fusing elements of reggae, ska, and rocksteady, as well as his distinctive vocal and songwriting style.
